The Problem

Meetings consume 15% of an organization's time, yet 73% of professionals multitask during them and action items are forgotten within 24 hours. Manual note-taking is unreliable, biased, and forces the note-taker out of active engagement. Most meetings end with no written record at all.

Our Solution

An intelligent, multilingual meeting assistant that automatically records audio, transcribes speech in English, Hindi, and Gujarati with speaker attribution, and generates structured summaries — turning every conversation into accountable, actionable documentation. Transcription runs fully offline for privacy.

Component Architecture

Audio Engine
SoundDevice capture at 16kHz mono, SciPy WAV encoding
ASR Engine
Faster Whisper with CTranslate2 backend, 3 model variants
Diarization
Resemblyzer d-vector embeddings, cosine similarity (0.80)
Indic Processor
Unicode normalization for Devanagari and Gujarati scripts
LLM Summarizer
Azure GPT-4.1 with structured prompt engineering
Export Module
Timestamped .txt output with transcript + summary sections

System Flow

1
User sets title, duration, language
2
Microphone captures audio at 16kHz mono
3
Audio saved as WAV, sent to Whisper for transcription
4
Each segment processed by Resemblyzer for speaker identification
5
Indic NLP normalizes Hindi/Gujarati text
6
Full transcript sent to Azure GPT-4.1 for structured summary
7
Output exported as timestamped .txt file

Key Features

Offline STT — Fully local transcription, no data leaves device
3 Languages — English, Hindi (Devanagari), Gujarati
Speaker ID — Automatic diarization up to 10 speakers
Timestamps — HH:MM:SS for every segment
AI Summary — Agenda, decisions, action items, recommendations
Portable .exe — Single-file deployment via PyInstaller
